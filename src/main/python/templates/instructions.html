Assessment Instructions

Please read carefully before starting your assessment

General Guidelines

Time Limit: You have 30 minutes to complete all 21 questions. The timer will start when you begin the assessment and cannot be paused.
  • Total Questions: 21 questions across 6 modules
  • Total Points: 100 points maximum
  • Passing Score: 70 points or higher
  • Attempts: One attempt per assessment session
  • Navigation: You can move forward through questions but cannot go back once submitted
  • Language: All questions are in English
Important: Ensure you have a stable internet connection and a quiet environment before starting. Avoid browser refresh or closing the tab during the assessment.

Assessment Rules

  1. Integrity: Complete the assessment independently without external help or resources.
  2. Environment: Find a quiet place with minimal distractions.
  3. Equipment: For speaking questions, ensure your microphone is working properly.
  4. Honesty: Do not use translation tools, dictionaries, or other resources during the assessment.
  5. Focus: Avoid switching browser tabs or windows during the assessment (this may be monitored).
  6. Submission: Each question is submitted individually and cannot be changed afterward.
Anti-Cheating Notice: This assessment includes monitoring features to ensure fairness. Suspicious behavior may result in assessment invalidation.

Scoring & Certification

To pass the assessment, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Total Score: Minimum 70 out of 100 points
  • Safety Questions: Minimum 80% correct on safety-related questions
  • Speaking Module: Minimum 12 out of 20 points

Certificate: Upon successful completion, you will receive a certificate valid for employment with Carnival Cruise Line. Your results will be available immediately after submission.

Technical Requirements

  • Browser: Google Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
  • Internet: Stable broadband connection (minimum 5 Mbps)
  • Microphone: Required for speaking module (built-in or external)
  • Audio: Headphones or speakers for listening module
  • Screen: Minimum 1024x768 resolution
