General Guidelines

Time Limit: You have 30 minutes to complete all 21 questions. The timer will start when you begin the assessment and cannot be paused.
  • Total Questions: 21 questions across 6 modules
  • Total Points: 100 points maximum
  • Passing Score: 70 points or higher
  • Attempts: One attempt per assessment session
  • Navigation: You can move forward through questions but cannot go back once submitted
  • Language: All questions are in English
Important: Ensure you have a stable internet connection and a quiet environment before starting. Avoid browser refresh or closing the tab during the assessment.
